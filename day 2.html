Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4cuo granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 eggg yoke
  • 1/4cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3cup dark brown sugar
  • 11/2tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. add the folur and mix
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. transfer the dought into the large boul
